    Belonging is a community effort. These organisations help students and educators build inclusive schools.

    • Headspace – Youth mental health support across Australia

    • Safe Schools Coalition – LGBTQ+ inclusion programs
    • Multicultural Youth Advocacy Network – Support for migrant and refugee young people
    • Foundation for Young Australians – Research on youth wellbeing and education
